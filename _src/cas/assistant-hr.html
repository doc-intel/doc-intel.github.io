One HR assistant for everything, triggering the right process

A recruiter or a hiring manager asks anything, without knowing which tool to open. An orchestrating assistant detects the intent and triggers the right flow: source candidates, query the pool, post a job, screen a CV, redact personal data.

The idea

You first build specialized assistants, each strong at one task: screening CVs, querying the candidate pool, drafting a posting, redacting personal data. Then one agentic assistant sits on top: it does not do everything itself, it understands the request and hands it to the right specialist. One entry point for the user; behind it, each process stays sharp.

How it works

Understand the requestThe question is parsed for its real intent, not just its words.
Route to the right specialistThe intent triggers the right flow: query, posting, screening, redaction.
Let the specialist runEach specialized assistant keeps its own tested logic and typed output.
Stay in the loopThe orchestrator follows the exchange and can re-route if the intent changes.

What it shows

An assistant that does everything is not one giant model guessing: it is an agentic orchestrator over solid specialized assistants. The value is in reliable intent routing and in the quality of each brick underneath.
